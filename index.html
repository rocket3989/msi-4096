The 4096 Ends of MSI

With 12 games left, there are 212, or 4096 different ways group play at the League of Legends Mid-Season Invitational can end. Three colors are used on the 4096 small squares to represent possible ends for a team in group stage- green for advancement, orange for tiebreaker, and red for elimination.

There are 6 grids below, one for each team in the group stage - IG, G2, SKT, TL, FW, PVB. Each grid is divided into 16, representing the 4 games the hovered team has influence over. Notice that in most cases, there is variance within these divisions, indicating that results from games outside of the control of the hovered team could determine if they advance or not.
